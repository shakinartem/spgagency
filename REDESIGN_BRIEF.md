# SPG Agency Redesign Brief

## Goal

Transform the current SPG Agency website into a premium medical growth agency website.

The website should feel:
- premium
- cinematic
- strategic
- editorial
- modern
- expensive

NOT:
- a clinic website
- a SaaS dashboard
- a startup template

---

## Niche

- dentistry
- medical clinics
- aesthetic medicine

---

## Positioning

SPG is a growth systems agency for modern clinics.

Focus:
- patient acquisition
- growth systems
- marketing infrastructure
- strategic consulting

---

## Visual Direction

Inspired by:
- BDSM Digital
- Montone Studio
- From Another

Use:
- oversized typography
- strong spacing rhythm
- dark luxury UI
- cinematic composition
- editorial brutalism
- warm accent colors
- subtle grain/noise
- modular layout system
- elegant motion

Avoid:
- healthcare corporate blue
- stock doctor imagery
- childish cards
- generic gradients
- overused SaaS patterns

---

## Priority

1. Layout hierarchy
2. Typography
3. Hero section
4. Section pacing
5. Motion language
6. Premium feeling

---

## Main Sections

1. Hero
