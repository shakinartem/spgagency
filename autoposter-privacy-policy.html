Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Autoposter, operated by SPG Agency, collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the platform or authorize access to your TikTok account.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the Autoposter website, login flow, dashboard, and related service functionality provided by SPG Agency. It also applies to information processed through the official TikTok API and TikTok OAuth authorization process where a user chooses to connect a TikTok account.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you use Autoposter, we may collect and process the following categories of data:

  • TikTok account basic profile information, such as account identifiers and display details.
  • OAuth authorization data, including authorization codes, access tokens, refresh tokens, and related token metadata.
  • Content metadata, such as titles, captions, publish timing, status labels, and workflow notes.
  • Publishing-related account data, including account connection state, sync timestamps, and API activity logs.
  • Technical data required for service operation, including browser information, request logs, and security events.

3. How we use information

Autoposter uses the information described above only as needed to provide the platform and its support functions.

  • Connect and authenticate TikTok accounts through the official TikTok OAuth flow.
  • Publish and manage video content on connected TikTok accounts.
  • Schedule content and maintain operational workflow states.
  • Display account and publishing status inside the dashboard.
  • Provide support, troubleshoot service issues, and maintain system security.

4. Legal basis and consent

By connecting a TikTok account and using Autoposter, you authorize SPG Agency to process the information necessary to operate the service. Access is limited to the permissions you explicitly grant through the TikTok OAuth consent screen and the scopes required for publishing and account connection.

5. No sale of personal data

SPG Agency does not sell personal data. Information obtained through Autoposter is used only to provide, operate, maintain, secure, and support the platform and its publishing workflow.

6. Data sharing

We do not share personal data with third parties except where necessary for service functionality, security, legal compliance, or operational support. Any processing by TikTok is governed by TikTok's own policies and terms.

7. Data retention

Autoposter retains data only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain audit history, resolve support requests, satisfy legal obligations, and protect the platform from abuse. Authentication data and content metadata may be retained while an account remains connected or while retention is required for legitimate operational reasons.

8. Security

SPG Agency uses commercially reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards to help protect data from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. These safeguards include access control, limited internal access, and secure handling of authorization tokens and related records.

9. Your choices

You may stop using Autoposter at any time and revoke TikTok authorization through the relevant TikTok account settings or by contacting us for assistance. When the service is disconnected, we will stop processing information that is no longer required for operation, subject to retention obligations.

10. Children

Autoposter is not intended for use by children who are not legally able to enter into agreements or use TikTok services on their own. If you believe a child has provided information to us, contact us so we can review the matter.

11. International use

Because Autoposter may be accessed from different locations, your information may be processed in jurisdictions where privacy laws differ. By using the service, you understand that data may be handled in the locations necessary to operate and support the platform.

12. Changes to this policy

SPG Agency may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect service changes, legal updates, or operational improvements. Updated versions will be posted on this page with a revised date.

13. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our handling of data in Autoposter, contact us at support@spgagency.ru.
